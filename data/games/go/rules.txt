## Go Game Rules

Go is an ancient two-player strategy board game. This implementation uses a 9×9 board (beginner size) with standard rules including ko and suicide prevention.

### 1. Setup & Preliminaries
- **Players:** 2 (Player 0 uses black 'B', Player 1 uses white 'W')
- **Board:** 9×9 grid of intersections
- **Komi:** White receives 6.5 points compensation for going second
- **Black plays first**

### 2. Basic Rules
- **Placement:** On your turn, place one stone on any empty intersection, or pass
- **Liberties:** A stone or connected group of stones survives if it has at least one liberty (adjacent empty point)
- **Capture:** If placing a stone removes all liberties from opponent stones, those stones are captured and removed

### 3. Special Rules
- **Suicide:** A move is illegal if it would cause your own stones to have no liberties (unless it captures opponent stones first)
- **Ko:** You cannot make a move that recreates the exact previous board position
- **Two passes:** Game ends when both players pass consecutively

### 4. Scoring (Area scoring)
- Count the number of intersections your stones occupy plus empty intersections your stones surround
- White adds komi (6.5 points)
- Higher score wins

### 5. Move Notation
- **place_R_C:** Place stone at row R, column C (0-indexed)
- **pass:** Pass your turn

### 6. Winning Conditions
- Game ends when both players pass consecutively
- Score is computed and higher score wins
- Winner gets +1.0, loser gets -1.0
- With komi at 6.5, ties are impossible

### Example Trajectory
This shows the beginning of a Go game.

Step 0: current_player=0, legal_actions=["place_0_0", "place_0_1", ..., "pass"], rewards=[0.0, 0.0]
  -> Action: "place_4_4"  # Black plays center (tengen)
Step 1: current_player=1, legal_actions=["place_0_0", ..., "pass"], rewards=[0.0, 0.0]
  -> Action: "place_2_6"  # White plays upper right
Step 2: current_player=0, legal_actions=[...], rewards=[0.0, 0.0]
  -> Action: "place_6_2"  # Black plays lower left
...
Step N-1: current_player=0, legal_actions=[...], rewards=[0.0, 0.0]
  -> Action: "pass"  # Black passes
Step N: current_player=1, legal_actions=[...], rewards=[0.0, 0.0]
  -> Action: "pass"  # White passes
Step N+1 (TERMINAL): current_player=-4, legal_actions=[], rewards=[-1.0, 1.0]
  # Game scored, White wins by 2.5 points
