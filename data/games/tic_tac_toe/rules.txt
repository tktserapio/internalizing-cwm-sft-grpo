### Rules of Tic-Tac-Toe
* **Setup:** Tic-Tac-Toe is played on a 3x3 grid, which starts completely empty.
* **Players and Marks:** There are two players: Player 0 uses the 'x' mark and Player
1 uses the 'o' mark.
* **Turns:** Player 0 ('x') always goes first, and turns alternate between players.
* **Making a Move:** On your turn, you must place your mark in a single, unoccupied
square. Attempting to place a mark in an already occupied square is an invalid move
; you must choose an empty square to complete your turn.
* **Winning the Game:** The winner is the first player to get three of their marks in
a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally). The game ends immediately as soon
as a winning line is formed.
* **Drawing the Game:** If all nine squares on the grid are filled and neither player
has won, the game ends in a draw.
* **End the Game:** The game only concludes upon a win or a draw. A player must make a move on their turn as long as there is at least one valid move available on the board. 
The winner earns reward of 1.0, and the loser earns reward of -1.0.
* **Move Notation:** Use the move notation 'mark(row,col)', where row and col are 0-indexed. For example, 'x(0,0)' means Player 0 ('x') places their mark in the topleft square.

### Example Trajectory
Step 0 (Player 0): The game begins on an empty 3x3 grid. It is Player 0's turn (playing as X). Choosing from all available empty cells on the board, Player 0 places their mark in the top-left corner at coordinate (0, 0).
Step 1 (Player 1): It is Player 1's turn (playing as O). Choosing only from the remaining empty cells, Player 1 places their mark in the middle-left cell at coordinate (1, 0).
Step 2 (Player 0): It is Player 0's turn again. Player 0 places their mark in the top-middle cell at coordinate (0, 1).
Step 3 (Player 1): Player 1 places their mark in the exact center of the board at coordinate (1, 1).
Step 4 (Player 0): Player 0 places their mark in the top-right corner at coordinate (0, 2).
Step 5 (Terminal): By successfully placing their third consecutive mark, Player 0 forms an unbroken horizontal line of 'X's across the entire top row of the grid. The game ends immediately. Player 0 receives a full win (+1.0), and Player 1 receives a loss (-1.0). No further actions can be taken.

## Action String Specification
Player actions: format "<mark>(<row>,<col>)" where mark ∈ {x, o}, row and col are 0-indexed integers
  - P0 uses mark "x": e.g. "x(0,0)", "x(1,2)", "x(2,2)"
  - P1 uses mark "o": e.g. "o(0,1)", "o(2,0)"
