GAME RULES: 2-PLAYER HANABI
================================================================================

DESCRIPTION
Hanabi is a cooperative card game where two players work together to build 
fireworks stacks of ascending numbers (1 through 5) in five distinct colors. 
The unique constraint is that players cannot see their own cards, only their 
partner's.

1. SETUP & PRELIMINARIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Players: 2 Players (IDs: 0 and 1).
- Deck: 50 cards total.
  - Suits: 5 (Red, Yellow, Green, Blue, White).
  - Ranks per Suit: Three 1s, two 2s, two 3s, two 4s, one 5.
- Tokens:
  - Info Tokens (Clues): Start with 8. Used to give hints.
  - Fuse Tokens (Lives): Start with 3. Lost when a wrong card is played.
- Initial State:
  - Deck is shuffled.
  - Each player is dealt 5 cards.
  - The board (fireworks display) starts empty (all colors at 0).
  - Discard pile is empty.

2. GAME MECHANICS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Turn Order]
- Players take turns sequentially (Player 0 -> Player 1 -> Player 0...).
- The game ends immediately if termination conditions are met.

[Observation Space]
- Public Information: The board state, discard pile, remaining fuse tokens, 
  remaining info tokens, and the *other* player's hand.
- Hidden Information: A player CANNOT see the rank or color of the cards in 
  their own hand.

[Legal Actions]
A player must perform exactly one of the following three actions on their turn:

A. Play a Card ("Play <card_index>")
   - The player chooses a card from their hand (indices 0-4).
   - SUCCESS: If the card is the next logical rank for its color (e.g., Red 3 
     onto Red 2), it is added to the board.
   - FAILURE: If the card does not fit, it is discarded, and the team loses 
     1 Fuse Token.
   - EFFECT: The player draws a replacement card (if deck is not empty).

B. Give Information ("Reveal ...")
   - Costs 1 Info Token. Illegal if Info Tokens are at 0.
   - The player points out specific attributes in their partner's hand.
   - Color Hint: "These cards are Red."
   - Rank Hint: "These cards are 5s."
   - CONSTRAINT: Must identify ALL cards matching that attribute.

C. Discard a Card ("Discard <card_index>")
   - The player chooses a card to discard to the trash pile.
   - BENEFIT: Regains 1 Info Token (cannot exceed 8).
   - EFFECT: The player draws a replacement card.
   - CONSTRAINT: Illegal if Info Tokens are already at maximum (8).

[State Transitions]
- Draw Deck: When a card is played or discarded, the hand is refilled 
  immediately. The new card enters at the last index position.
- Fuse Logic: If Fuses drop to < 0 (the 3rd strike), the game terminates.

3. TERMINATION & SCORING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[End Conditions]
1. Victory: All 5 colors have stacks completed up to rank 5 (Score 25).
2. Defeat: The 3rd Fuse token is lost.
3. Deck Exhaustion: The deck runs out. Players get one final round of turns.

[Rewards/Scoring]
- Score: The sum of the highest rank in each of the 5 color stacks.
- Range: 0 to 25.
- Penalty: If the game ends via Defeat (3 fuses lost), score is 0.

4. MOVE NOTATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The following action strings are observed in trajectories:

- Play: "Play <index>" 
  (e.g., "Play 0" plays the card in the 0th slot of the hand).
  
- Discard: "Discard <index>" 
  (e.g., "Discard 4").
  
- Hint Color: "Reveal color <player_id> <color>" 
  (e.g., "Reveal color 1 R" tells Player 1 about all their Red cards).
  
- Hint Rank: "Reveal rank <player_id> <rank>" 
  (e.g., "Reveal rank 0 5" tells Player 0 about all their 5s).

EXAMPLE TRAJECTORY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Trajectory ID: game_log_2p_001
Result: Score 22 (Game ended by empty deck)

STEP 0: Initial Deal
- State: Board Empty. Clues: 8. Lives: 3.
- P0 Hand: [R1, B3, G1, W5, R2] (Hidden from P0)
- P1 Hand: [Y1, Y1, G2, B4, W1] (Hidden from P1)

STEP 1: Player 0 moves
- Action: "Reveal rank 1 1"
- Effect: P0 tells P1 that cards at index 0 and 1 are Rank 1.
- Clues drop to 7.

STEP 2: Player 1 moves
- Action: "Play 0"
- Effect: P1 plays index 0 (Yellow 1). Success. P1 draws Red 5.
- Board: [Y1]

STEP 3: Player 0 moves
- Action: "Play 2"
- Effect: P0 plays index 2 (Green 1). Success. P0 draws Blue 1.
- Board: [Y1, G1]

STEP 4: Player 1 moves
- Action: "Discard 4"
- Effect: P1 discards index 4 (Red 5) to regain a clue. P1 draws Green 5.
- Clues rise to 8.

## Action String Specification
Player actions:
  - "Play <index>": play card at 0-indexed hand position, e.g. "Play 0", "Play 3"
  - "Discard <index>": discard card at 0-indexed hand position, e.g. "Discard 1"
  - "Reveal color <player> <color>": give color clue, e.g. "Reveal color 1 Red"
  - "Reveal rank <player> <rank>": give rank clue, e.g. "Reveal rank 0 3"
