Objective: The goal is to be the first player to occupy the Center Square (2, 2).

Setup:

Board: A 5x5 grid with coordinates (row, col) from (0,0) to (4,4).

Player 0 (Blue): Starts with two units at (0, 0) and (0, 4).

Player 1 (Red): Starts with two units at (4, 0) and (4, 4).

Movement:

Players take turns moving one of their units.

A unit can move one step in any direction (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to an adjacent empty square.

No Captures: You cannot move onto a square occupied by any unit (friend or foe).

The "Stun" Mechanic:

If a player moves a unit to a square adjacent (orthogonally or diagonally) to an opponent's unit, the opponent's unit becomes Stunned.

A Stunned unit cannot move on the owner's next turn.

Stun status lasts for exactly one turn. (It clears after the opponent's turn passes).

If a player has no legal moves (e.g., all units are stunned or blocked), they must Pass.

Winning:

Victory: The first player to move a unit onto the Center Square (2, 2) wins instantly.

Draw: If 50 turns pass without a winner, the game is a draw.

## Action String Specification
Player actions:
  - Move: format "move (r1,c1) to (r2,c2)" where r,c are 0-indexed integers
    e.g. "move (0,0) to (1,1)", "move (2,1) to (2,2)"
  - "pass": pass the turn
